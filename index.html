HOW IT WORKS

When you make a maintenance request, the platform instantly connects you with an expert technician who performs the service right at your location and on your schedule.

1

Customers order through the mobile, tablet or desktop platform.

2

A nearby expert technician receives the request.

3

The platform sends you updates leading up to the service as well as when the technician executes the maintenance at your location.

4

Once the job is complete, the platform generates an invoice for quick and easy payment.
